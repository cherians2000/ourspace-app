# OurSpace Product Requirements Document (PRD)

Version: 1.0
Status: Locked
Last Updated: July 2026

---

# 1. Vision

OurSpace is a private digital space shared between two people where they can communicate, express emotions, collaborate, preserve memories, and strengthen their relationship through shared experiences.

The goal is not to replace messaging apps like WhatsApp.

The goal is to create a shared place that feels alive.

---

# 2. Mission

Help people build stronger relationships through shared digital experiences.

---

# 3. Target Users

Version 1 focuses on:

- Couples
- Married partners
- Best friends
- Family members

Version 2 may include:

- Group Spaces
- Study groups
- Startup teams
- Communities

---

# 4. Core Concept

Every relationship exists inside a Space.

A Space is private.

A user can have multiple Spaces.

Version 1 allows only two members per Space.

The database is designed to support more members in future versions.

# 6. Not Included in Version 1

- Voice Calls
- Video Calls
- Group Spaces
- AI Assistant
- Premium Subscription
- Payments
- Stories
- Games

---

# 7. Product Principles

Every feature should increase human connection.

Everything should feel calm.

Every important relationship deserves its own Space.

Chat is one feature inside a Space—not the center of the app.
